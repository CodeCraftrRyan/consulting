Bio

I came to data the long way through museums, classrooms, and mission-driven organizations that needed to run better.

For more than a decade, I have built data, CRM, and integrated technology systems that nonprofits depend on. I currently serve as a director of data and technology at a national nonprofit, where I built the data function from the ground up.

Earlier, I worked on the operations side of the sector: program management in environmental education, education center direction and public programs at a major national museum, and volunteer leadership at a local historical society.

I hold an M.A. in History from Simmons University, with a focus in public history. I also completed public history training through the American Association for State and Local History StEPs program.
